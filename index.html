About Us

Clean and Healthy New York is an environmental health advocacy organization committed to building a just and healthy society where toxic chemicals are unthinkable.



We are proud to provide leadership to two major statewide disease-prevention coalitions: the JustGreen Partnership, a team of 50 organizations representing over a million New Yorkers, and Lead Free Kids New York, a coalition of individuals and organizations working to end childhood lead exposure in New York. Our work has been central to numerous flagship, nation-leading efforts to protect environmental health.
